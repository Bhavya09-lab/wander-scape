Melodies of Gujarat – A Musical Journey

Gujarat’s music is deeply rooted in its folk traditions. From devotional Garba tunes in Vadodara to soulful Bhajans of Dwarka, every city offers a unique rhythm that echoes culture, tradition, and celebration.

Folk Singers of Gujarat

Kirtidan Gadhvi

One of Gujarat's most beloved folk singers, known for his powerful renditions of Dayro and Bhajans that stir deep emotions.

Geeta Rabari

Popularly known as the “Kutchi Koyal,” Geeta Rabari brings vibrant folk traditions to the youth through her soulful voice.

Jignesh Kaviraj

A celebrated singer in Gujarati folk and DJ-style Garba fusion songs, widely loved across generations.

Hemant Chauhan

Renowned for his devotional and spiritual songs, Hemant Chauhan’s voice is a staple in temples and Gujarati households.

Ishani Dave

A youth sensation known for modern Garba hits, Ishani brings a contemporary flavor to traditional music styles.

Aishwarya Majmudar

Winner of multiple singing competitions, her voice ranges from classical to devotional to modern Gujarati film music.

Rajbha gadhavi

Veteran folk and Dayro performer, well-known for his satirical and spiritual storytelling through songs.

Praful Dave

Veteran folk and Dayro performer, well-known for his satirical and spiritual storytelling through songs.

Aditya gadhavi

Aditya Gadhvi is a celebrated Gujarati singer and lyricist known for blending folk, Sufi, and devotional music with a modern twist. His hits like “Khalasi” and “Naagar Nandji Na Laal” have gained global recognition. A student of A.R. Rahman's conservatory, he has captivated audiences worldwide.
